Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below, each with a hidden value. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a certain number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will change.
